An Interview with Amanda Burns

Sunday, November 9, 2020

Stephanie: What part of design do you enjoy the most and why?

Amanda: I enjoy illustration and taking my illustrations and being able to print them in a useful matter, or just being able to hold my final product.

S: What got you first interested in design and when?

A: I've always had an interest in art, but in grade 11 I did a screen-printing project and that's when I found a love for print.

S: What are you hoping to do with design once you graduate from NC?

A: I hope to work in a print shop that does screen printing, maybe do a bit of free-lance but I'm not sure yet.

S: What aspects make a website functional and pleasing to look at?

A: I think readability and spacing, along with a creative element to make it pop/interesting to look at makes it functional.

S: How important do you think having a well designed website is for a business and why?

A: I think it's very important, especially in today's society where most things are now online. Having a functional website might be your only way to get your name or product out (especially with Covid lockdown).

S: What is your favourite and least favourite part about web design?

A: My favourite part would be designing a layout and applying images. My least favourite would be the html because it's a learning curve and takes a while to get the hang of it.
